VQA & Visual
📝摘要
We introduce SpaRT-QA, a novel benchmark for evaluating spatial reasoning capabilities of vision-language models. The dataset contains 50K questions requiring understanding of 3D spatial relationships, object occlusion, and perspective reasoning. Our analysis reveals that even the strongest VLMs achieve only 62% accuracy, highlighting a significant gap in current models' spatial reasoning abilities.
